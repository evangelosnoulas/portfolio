Performance Management-Metro Deutschland

For the course “Performance Management”, we took over a project where me and my team collaborated (unofficially) with Metro Deutschland to work on a challenge relevant to their performance management system. Specifically, the challenge was about poor communication of the goals from managers to the lower-level employees. Thus, employees had an unclear image of the tasks required from them and managers did not have clear KPIs to evaluate employees’ performance. In this project, me and my team, after an interview with the head of Metro’s HR department, we wrote a consultancy report to analyze the situation and provide Metro’s HR manager with clear advice to tackle this problem.

Field & Acquired skills: Consultancy skills, Academic writing skills, Teamwork, Project management, Performance Management

Recruitment & Selection-Redesign of AVIs

In the course Recruitment and Selection, we were given the opportunity to redesign the tool of Asynchronous Video Interviews to enhance its validity and to eliminate potential discrimination from the tool against minority groups. After a thorough examination of the existing literature, we came up with specific advise for the achievement of these two goals and then, we presented our paper to a recruiter from NS, the Dutch railways company.

Field & Acquired skills: Communication and Presentation skills, Teamwork, Research skills, Academic writing, Recruitment & Selection

Talent Development & Analytics – Coolblue

For the course “Talent Development and Analytics”, me and my team did a group project with Coolblue as a part of an official collaboration. The goal of this project was to write a consultancy report for Coolblue, on how it can restructure its offices to facilitate transition from home office to the real office after Covid-19, so that employees can feel safe, physically and mentally well, motivated and engaged. Furthermore, another part of the challenge was to assist Coolblue’s employees to combat professional isolation during Covidd-19 pandemic. During the whole project, we had several meetings with employees from Coolblue, to understand better the culture of the company, their aspirations and to discuss about our ideas and progress. Our assignment was awarded the 2nd position and was graded with an 8.

Field & Acquired skills: Consultancy skills, Research Skills, Networking skills, Teamwork, Project Management, Talent Development & Analytics

Talent Development and Analytics - Management advisory report

Also, for the course “Talent Development and Analytics”, I did an individual assignment where I compared fictitious data in excel from 4 training programs to find which would be the most effective and with the best Return of Investment. All these trainings were presented in a management advisory report for Tesla (as a fictitious case) to develop its employees’ performance on its call center. This assignment was awarded a grade of 8,3.

Field & Acquired skills:Consultancy skills, Training and Development of employees, Analytical Skills (Analysis with Excel)

Master’s Thesis

The Master’s Thesis project was one of the most challenging and at the same time, interesting things that I have done during my studies. It was a valuable experience because I learnt how to build a research project from scratch, how to overcome problems that may arise, deal with setbacks and be more persistent. Also, I was in charge of managing everything relevant to the project such as time management, find a company and conducting interviews with its employees. I wrote a qualitative thesis on Human Energy at work and specifically on “how do employees manage and sustain their energy in a high-pressure work context”.

Field & Acquired skills:Academic writing skills, Research skills, Project Management skills, Deeper understanding about employees’ well-being and human energy, Organizing skills, Persistence.
